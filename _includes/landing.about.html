A platform for


 

Seamless access to cloud computing

Your data will be automatically stored in cloud and computed on a variety of cloud computing resources without you manually managing those resources.
 

Publishing brain data and derivatives

You can upload brain data and generate data derivatives and we will issue a DOI so that you can publish your data and apps together through our platform.
 

Reproducible neuroscience research

You can safely archive your dataset and use properly versioned Apps that can be executed reliably on our resources with full data provenance.

Register Apps

You can register your algorithms on Brainlife and let other users discover them.
 

Upload Datasets

You can upload your datasets and organize them inside your project. You can share them publicly, or with your project members.
 

Run Apps

You can submit and monitor App and share results simultanously with other users. Generated datasets can be archived and used to run other Apps.
