An online platform for reproducible neuroscience.

Apps

A software developers can publish their code on github and register them as "Apps" on Brainlife for other users to discover and execute them on their data.

Data

Neuroimaging data can be published and shared with other users to be analyzed by Apps on Brainlife.

Computing Resources

Launch Apps on our shared cloud resources, or you can register your computing resources to accelerate selected Apps and share it with other users.

Analyze "Big Data"

Brainlife is capable of analyzing large number of subjects (>1000) and their derivatives through our meta-job orchestration engine.

You can easily handle thousands of tasks submitted by our rule based task orghestration UI.

Visualize

Brainlife provides variety of visualization tools. Both native (fsleyes, freeview, etc..) and web-based Apps are supported.

Discover Apps and Analyze

Try the Brainlife Apps. Apps are open cloud services. Use the Apps to easily analyze data, monitor progress, visualize your results and share them with other users.

Provenance

Brainlife keeps track of all analysis performed on your data and make sure your outputs are reproducible.

Publish

Publish datasets and data derivaties along with all the Apps used to generated them using a single DOI. The published data will be archived on our secure tape drives for long term curation.

Our publication mechianism allows your collaborators to easily reuse your research products.
