Brainlife is for Everyone!

Brailife reaches out beyond neuroscience. It allows computer scientists, statisticians and engineers interested in brain data to use the data to publish their methods and get credit for their work.




Psychologists and neroscientists

Use cloud computing to understand the brain and behavior.


Computer scientists and engineers

Use published data and applications to develop algorithms and computing methods.


Statisticians and data scientists

Use available data and derivatives to gather new insights into individuality and variability in human populations.
